What Is Football?

Football (sometimes called SOCCER). A game played on a rectangular field between 2 teams consisting of 11 players each on the field at all times. Each with the objective of scoring the most goals before the end of the match. Goals are scored by kicking or hitting a ball with any part of the body except their arms or hands.

  • A game of football starts with a coin toss.
  • Winner chooses whether they start with the ball or not.
  • The team with the most goals by the end of the game wins.

Positions:

The main categories of positions in football are: Defenders, Midfielders, Forwards, and Goalkeepers.

Defenders:
-Defenders protect the goal by maintaining strategic poitions and stopping oncoming attacks.
-They need to have good stamina, strength, and agility to stop oncoming attacks.
-Defenders also need to be good communicators to work together to organize the defense.

Midfielders:
-Midfielders control the center of the field. Their main job is to control the ball and move it up the field.
-They need to be strong it both attacking and defending.
-They assist in both attacking and defending, setting up attacks, and preventing attacks from reaching defenders.

Forwards:
-Also known as strikers, a forward's main objective is goal scoring.
-These players go as far down the field as needed to score goals.
-Responsible for making attacks to allow for goal scoring.
Goalkeepers:
-The only player allowed to touch the ball during the game.
-Their sole objective is to keep the ball from entering the net.
-They are not allowed to touch the ball with their hands if they are outside their goalbox.

Goalkeepers

Role:

A goalkeeper (sometimes called the goalie, keeper, or goaltender) is the last line of defense in a game of football. Often goalies are the most athletic on the team, as they need to be quick athletic, and have storng hands. Goalies need to have sure hands as they will need to learn to catch all kinds of balls. Just one mistake could cost a goal, maybe the game.

Stopping balls:

Stopping a rolling ball sounds easy, but the ball could have a particular spin or strange bounce making it harder to stop. Stopping a ball in the air can also be tricky. They can curve, dive, and just move erratically because of their spin, lack of spin, or speed. Another option to stop a ball is to just block it. Either by hitting it away, punching, or even kicking. However, goalies must be careful not to deflect in the direction of an opposing team. Sometimes goalies dive on the ground to deflect rolling balls, and sometimes they need to jump to deflect high balls in the air.

Communication:

The goalkeeper needs to be able to communicate and cooperate with the defenders. Since the goalie has the best view of the field, he/she can call out any unmarked players or approaching players. The goalie can be seen as the director of the defense.


Defenders

Role:

An outfield player who's primary rol is to stop attacks, and prevent the opposing team from scoring goals.

Passing:

Defenders should be good at passing, to be able to keep the ball away from the opponent with precise and well positioned passes.

Communication:

Defenders should be good at communicating as they will move around a lot when the opposing team attacks. They should be able to communicate with the goalie and with eachother.


Midfielders

Role:

Midfielders assist with both attacking and defending. They remain near the ceneter of the field and either assist with attacking or defending depending on what the situation calls for. There are certain midfielder roles that are primarily for defending and roles primarily for attacking. Such as: CDM, and CAM.

Communication:

They need excellent communication because they could sometimes be attacking and sometimes defending. They need to be able to communicate with both the defenders and forwards for both defending and attacking.


Forwards

Role:

A forward/attackers main role is to score goals. They are responsible for attacking and goal scoring, which is essential to win a game of football.

Communication

Forwards need to have good communication in order to set up attacks, as usually they will need to adapt on the field.
